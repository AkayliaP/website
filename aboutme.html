Akaylia is a Jamaican artist currrently residing in New York.
As a child, the beautiful images she saw from various Pixar and Disney flims inspired her to start drawing.
She enjoys watching stop motion films/series(Fantastic Mr.Fox,The House,ParaNorman,The PJs),reading comics, and hanging out with her close friends.
While she has been illustrating for most of her life, 3D modeling has peaked her interest and she has completed four projects in Cinema 4D (projects she's not yet ready to show).
Akaylia is currently attending CUNY CCNY full time while working.
